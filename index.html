METAL BUILDING PURCHASE AGREEMENT

This Agreement is entered into between Burnett Custom Homes ("Contractor") and the customer ("Owner") identified in this contract.

1. SCOPE OF WORK

Contractor agrees to provide and install a metal building according to the specifications selected by Owner, including the package type, size, and any add-ons as detailed in this agreement.

2. PRICING AND PAYMENT

The total contract price is as shown in the order summary. Payment terms: 50% deposit upon signing, 25% at start of construction, 25% upon completion.

3. TIMELINE

Construction will commence within 30 days of permit approval and is estimated to take 45-90 days depending on size and complexity.

Step 7: Contract Terms - Warranties and Responsibilities

4. WARRANTIES

Metal panels and structure carry a manufacturer's warranty as specified in package details. Contractor warrants workmanship for one year from completion date.

5. PERMITS AND INSPECTIONS

Contractor will obtain necessary building permits. Owner is responsible for property survey if required by local jurisdiction.

6. SITE PREPARATION

Owner shall provide a level, accessible site with adequate drainage. Additional site preparation may incur extra charges.

7. CHANGES AND MODIFICATIONS

Any changes to the agreed specifications must be documented in writing and may affect pricing and timeline.

Step 8: Contract Terms - Final Provisions

8. CANCELLATION

Either party may cancel this agreement with written notice. Cancellation by Owner after materials are ordered may result in forfeiture of deposit.

9. LIABILITY AND INSURANCE

Contractor maintains general liability insurance. Owner should verify their property insurance covers the structure during and after construction.

10. DISPUTE RESOLUTION

Any disputes shall be resolved through mediation before pursuing legal action. Applicable law is that of the state where the property is located.

11. ENTIRE AGREEMENT

This contract represents the entire agreement between parties and supersedes all prior discussions or agreements.
